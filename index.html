This tool lets you change the key that controls your XRPL wallet — without moving your XRP, NFTs, or tokens anywhere.
HOW IT WORKS
01 Connect — Choose Mainnet or Testnet
02 Load your compromised wallet — Enter the seed or phrase for the account you want to secure.
03 Set the new key — Generate a fresh wallet or use one you already have.
04 Broadcast — Sends the rekey transaction to the XRP Ledger.
05 Disable old key — Permanently locks out the old compromised seed.
⚠ Your secrets never leave your device. All signing happens locally in your browser. Never share your seed or secret numbers with anyone.
